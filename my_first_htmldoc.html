Introucing Golf Place

Golfplace is a brand new place where young golfers can come to learn and to improve their gold skills.
The site will be a place to start and contiue conversations that anyone is able to participate in.

There are several goals we want to acheive:

  • Allow people to learn golf
  • Give people a place to share stories
  • Give people the resources learn on their own

In order to meet these goals we have several rules:

  • Be respectful
  • And most importantly Be Happy!
